✅ Online Personal Training

Customized Workout Plans: You will receive new workouts and exercises every week! Tailored exercise routines based on your individual needs and goals.

Progress Tracking: You will be provided with tools to monitor performance, track results, and make adjustments as needed.

Communication Channels: We will share direct interaction through messaging, calls, and video calls. Staying on track with support and encouragement from me!

Technique Correction: All my client’s submit videos of all their workouts for feedback on form and technique.

Mobile Learning: I will be providing the focus or importance of every exercise! Ability to access training materials, questions, and resources on things you want or need to learn.

✅ Online Nutrition Coaching

Personalized Meal Plans: You will receive new meal plans every week! Tailored to your individual needs, preferences, and goals. Often including mostly macro targets, metabolism focus, energy levels and meals that aim to help you enjoy the process of eating healthy.

Nutritional Education: You will learn about macronutrients, micronutrients, portion control, food labeling, and healthy food choices. As well as, understanding how food affects the body and how to make informed choices!

Habit Formation: We will work towards developing sustainable eating patterns and strategies for long-term success. Such as, overcoming emotional eating, late night cravings, and other obstacles to healthy eating.

Ongoing Support: I will do regular check-ins, feedback, and adjustments to the plan as needed.

Progress Tracking: Monitoring weight, body composition, and other relevant markers.

Accountability: Staying on track with support and encouragement from me!
