✅ Online Personal Training

Online Personal Training

  • Customized Workout Plans: You will receive new workouts and exercises every week—tailored specifically to your individual needs and goals.
  • Progress Tracking: Tools to monitor performance, track your results, and make adjustments as needed.
  • Communication Channels: Direct interaction through messaging, calls, and video calls for support, guidance, and accountability.
  • Technique Correction: All clients submit workout videos for detailed feedback on form and technique to ensure proper execution and maximize results.
  • Mobile Learning: I’ll provide the key information, resources, and personal knowledge you need to learn, grow, and stay consistent.
  • Digital Workouts: My software application includes every exercise, rep count, weight selection, speed/tempo, rest time, and focus point—along with a correctly displayed demonstration video for each movement.
✅ Online Nutrition Coaching

Online Nutrition Coaching

  • Personalized Meal Plans: New meal plans every week, tailored to your needs and preferences (often including macro targets, metabolism support, and energy levels).
  • Nutrition Education: Learn about macros, micros, portion sizes, labels, and ingredients—plus how specific foods affect your body so you can make informed choices.
  • Habit Formation: Build sustainable eating patterns and overcome challenges like emotional eating or drinking, late‑night snacking, and sugar cravings.
  • Ongoing Support: Regular check‑ins, feedback, and personalized adjustments.
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor weight, body composition, body‑fat %, muscle‑mass %, and more.
