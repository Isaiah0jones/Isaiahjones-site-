✅ Online Personal Training

  • Customized Workout Plans: You will receive new workouts and exercises every week! Tailored routines based on your individual needs and goals.
  • Progress Tracking: Tools to monitor performance, track results, and adjust as needed.
  • Communication Channels: Direct interaction through messaging, calls, and video calls for support and accountability.
  • Technique Correction: All my clients submit videos of their workouts for feedback on form and technique.
  • Mobile Learning: I’ll provide the importance, resources, and personal knowledge for what you want and need to learn.
  • Digital Workouts: My software application will include every exercise, rep, weight, speed, rest time, and focus, along with a correctly displayed video of the exercise.

✅ Online Nutrition Coaching

  • Personalized Meal Plans: New meal plans every week tailored to your needs and preferences (often including macro targets, metabolism focus, and energy levels).
  • Nutritional Education: Learn about macros, micros, portion control, labels, and ingredients. Most importantly, how certain food affects your body a certain way so you can make informed choices.
  • Habit Formation: Build sustainable eating patterns and overcome challenges like emotional eating or drinking, late‑night cravings, or sweet cravings.
  • Ongoing Support: Regular check‑ins, feedback, and adjustments.
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor weight, body composition, body fat %, muscle mass % and more.
